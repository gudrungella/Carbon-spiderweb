About

Carbon Spiderweb tracks the embodied and operational emissions of your telecommunication network with automatic data quality, sensitivity and uncertainty checks. The tool runs entirely in your browser — your data never physically leaves your device, nor do the results (source code).

Why Carbon Spiderweb?

📊
Instant carbon report
Generates a full emissions breakdown with charts that pinpoint the hotspots in your network at component, site, and network level.
🖱️
Easy to use
No installation, no coding. Fill in the Excel templates, upload them, click Run. Works in any modern browser.
🔬
Peer-reviewed methodology
Built on published research. Embodied, operational, and end-of-life emissions follow a documented, citable approach.
🔒
Secure by design
All computation happens in your browser via WebAssembly. No data is sent to any server — ever.
🌐
Industry-aligned
Uses the same methodology adopted by other network operators, enabling meaningful cross-operator comparisons.
🔄
Year-over-year tracking
Consistent inputs and methodology make results fully reproducible, so you can track progress and meet reporting obligations reliably.
📉
Measure your reductions
Sensitivity analysis reveals which changes — equipment swaps, energy mix, lifetime extensions — have the greatest impact on your footprint.
